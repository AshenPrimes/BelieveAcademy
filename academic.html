GENERAL INFORMATION:

We offer the following curricula:

  1. CIE (Cambridge International Examinations):
    • British Cambridge Curriculum.
    • Provides an international matric certificate accepted in numerous countries abroad.
    • Accepted by all universities in South Africa
    • We are also a CambriLearn affiliated tutor centre for parents choosing this curriculum provider.
  2. GED (General Educational Diploma):
    • American General Educational Diploma.
    • We are affiliated with GETEDUCARE, offering timetables, schedules, manuals, scheduled on-line classes, pre-recorder video’s, assessments, quizzes, pre-tests, exam papers etc to help prepare the students for their final exams. Note: Not all South African universities recognize GED as entry-level, but it is recognized by tertiary educations institutions such as UNISA. (A list of these institutions is available)

What we expect from students:

  • Take responsibility for your own learning! Teachers open the door, but YOU have to enter by yourself!!
  • ASK when you don’t understand! We will find out together if necessary!
  • Work diligently and hard.
  • Set daily goals for yourself and stick to them. Work at home if you don’t finish at school. Make use of resources like the internet / library to help you master concepts that you struggle with.
  • Study work on a regular basis and make notes when the subject specialists are there. Ask if you need help.
  • Assess your own work regularly and do corrections. Use sticky notes to mark concepts that you do not understand. Ask the subject specialists to explain these concepts when they come to class.
  • If you need help with time management, setting of goals etc, please ask!
